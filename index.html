Our project flow

1. Brainstorming

Brainstorming is a sort of preparation which consists of a lot of research and workshops.

2. Evaluation

During this stage we start exploring the chosen technologies and evaluate them.

3. MODEL

The model is a functional prototype, with self-made hardware, software, enclosure, wearable parts, etc.

4. Prototype

The prototype is a revised version of the model and it already has a design similar to the final product.

5. Product

Usually the final product is optimized for mass-production and often made with a post-production tester.
